Meet Your Instructor

Kirsten originally stepped onto the mat to improve her surfing while she was raised on the island of Oahu, but over time grew to passionately love the specific practice of Yoga Sculpt. With her eccentric, spunky attitude she decided to take it to the next level and become certified in 2015.

Yoga Sculpt

Yoga Sculpt is a fusion of Vinyasa Yoga and HIIT incorporated with light weights. All levels of yoga are welcome!
